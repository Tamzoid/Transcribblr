Translate Japanese → English
Runs the C3TR-Adapter (Gemma-2-9B + PEFT) over every transcribed record and fills in the English lane. Records still containing ???? are skipped, and records that already have an English translation are kept as-is unless you tick "re-translate" below.
Leave blank for literal translation. slang = casual phrasing (e.g. "heads-up" instead of "warning"); nsfw = allows adult content; both can be combined.
