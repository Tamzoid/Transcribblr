Bulk clear
Both actions touch every record in this project. They do not delete the records themselves or the timing — only the text fields. Speakers, scenes, characters, and the rest of the project context are untouched.
Clear all translations
Removes the English translation, literal lane, translator notes, and the 🆕 review flag from every record. Keeps Japanese + romaji intact.
Clear all down to ????
Reverts every record to its untranscribed state — Japanese back to ????, romaji + English + literal + notes wiped. The transcribe pipeline can re-fill them. Destructive: cannot be undone without a project ZIP backup.
Conversational transcription review (Qwen2.5-14B)
Pick transcribed records and chat with the model to spot likely Whisper errors — homophone confusion, broken grammar, wrong particles. Click Apply on a reply to write its corrected Japanese back to the project. Updates only text.ja (romaji is regenerated automatically).

Guess speakers (Qwen2.5-14B)

  

    Walks every transcribed record that has no speaker assigned and asks the
    model to guess based on the character roster, scene context, story
    summary, and surrounding lines. Each guess lands as a suggestion
    that you can accept or dismiss either here or directly on the Records →
    Speakers sub-tab. Suggestions don't overwrite manually-set speakers.
